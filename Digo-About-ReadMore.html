What is Forex Marketing?

Foreign Exchange market(FOREX,FX) is an institution for the exchange of one
country's currency with that of another country.

Foreign Exchange markets are made up of many different markets,
because each inter-trades constitutes a market.

Brief History on Forex Marketing?

The first Forex Market was established in Amsterdam,roughly 500 years ago.
The Forex Market is the largest financial market world wide.

This possibilities to freely trade currencies helped to stabilize currency
exchange rates.From Amsterdam,Forex Trades throughout the whole world
were initiated.
