What is Trading-Strategy?

Trading-Strategy,is a systematic methodology used for buying
and selling in the securities market.Trading-Strategies are
either simple or complex.

Trading-Strategies are based on predefined rules and criteria
used when making trading decisions.Trading strategies involves
considerations such as investment style{eg,value}.

Brief History on Trading-Strategy.

Most Trading Trading-Strategies are based on either technical analysis
or fundamental analysis,which are informed by quantifiable and verifiable
market information.

Strategies that rely on technical indicators tend to focus on market
strikes and their movements.
