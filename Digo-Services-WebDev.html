What is WebDevelopment?

Web-Development is the building and maintenance of websites.Web-devs,
do this by using a variety of coding language.

Web-Development happens to be the work behind the scenes to make a,
website look great,work fast and perform well with a seamless user
experience.

Brief History on WebDevelopment

Tim Berners-lee,a british scientist,invented the World Wide Web in 1889
while working at CERN.

This Web was originally conceived and developed to meet the demand for
automated information sharing between scientists in universities and
institutes around the world.
